About Us


Since 2017

The Cafe was founded by a group of friends from college for a place to sit and have loads of Conversations and Coffee. It ended up being a huge hit among people and later became a full fledged cafe. Now, we serve a lot of variety drinks and shakes. We only use seasonal ingredients.

In addition to our full espresso and juice bar menu, we serve a variety of fresh pastries. We hope you enjoy it as much as we do. "Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, not too late." Fresh is the new sweet.
